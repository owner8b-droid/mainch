Services

From first site visit to final stamped drawing — here's how we work, and what we cover.

01 / Structural

Structural Design & Review

Design and analysis of steel, concrete, and timber structures for new builds, additions, and retrofits. Includes load calculations, connection design, and construction documents.

02 / Geotechnical

Geotechnical Studies

Soil boring and lab testing, foundation recommendations, slope stability, and retaining wall design based on real site conditions.

03 / Infrastructure

Infrastructure & Site Civil

Roadway, drainage, grading, and utility design for residential, commercial, and municipal infrastructure projects.

04 / Inspection

Construction Inspection

On-site inspection and quality assurance during construction to confirm the work matches the stamped design.

05 / Permitting

Permitting & Code Review

Preparation and submission of engineering documents for municipal permitting, with code compliance review.

06 / Forensic

Forensic & Assessment

Investigation of existing structures — damage assessment, cause-of-failure analysis, and repair recommendations.

How a project moves

From site visit to stamped set.

01

Site visit & scope

We walk the site, gather existing conditions, and define exactly what needs to be engineered.

02

Analysis & calculations

Loads, soil data, and code requirements are run through calculation before any drawing starts.

03

Drawings & documentation

Calculations become construction documents — drawings, details, and specifications ready for permitting.

04

Permitting support

We respond to plan-check comments and revise documents until the permit is issued.

05

Construction inspection

We visit the site during key milestones to confirm construction matches the design.
